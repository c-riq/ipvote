Vote Delegation

Chris Rieckmann | February 17, 2025

Introduction

Vote delegation, also known as liquid democracy or proxy voting, allows participants to delegate their voting power to trusted individuals who may have more expertise or time to evaluate specific issues. This feature increases the ranges of issues that can be addressed in IP-based and phone number-based polling by combining elements of direct and representative democracy.

Key Benefits:

  • Enables more informed decision-making through expert delegation
  • Reduces voter fatigue on complex or numerous issues
  • Maintains democratic participation while leveraging expertise
  • Allows for dynamic adjustment of delegation preferences

How Vote Delegation Works

The delegation system operates on several key principles:

  • Voluntary delegation to trusted individuals or organizations
  • Topic-specific delegation options (e.g., environmental, economic, or technical issues)
  • Ability to override delegation for specific polls
  • Transparent delegation chains and voting history
  • Revocable delegation at any time

How do we ensure integrity?

We allow the poll results to be aggregated based on different identity sources. Where each identifier is counted as 1 vote:

  • IP address
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Twitter handle

With increasing impact of polls, the ecomonic and political incentives to manipulate polls, may require adding additional identity sources.
