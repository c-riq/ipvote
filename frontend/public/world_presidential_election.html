World Presidential Election

Chris Rieckmann | February 5, 2025

Introduction

The World Presidential Election is a global poll to elect the next World President. Every verified phone number gets one vote and one vote can be submitted per IP-address. Initially there will be montly poll evaluations at the end of each month by Rix Dat NL B.V. to determine the president for the next month. The president will get to decide on the usage of 1000 EUR during their term.

Election criteria:

  • Only votes on the open poll "Who should be world president?" are considered
  • Candidates need to be specified via a URL that uniquely identifies the person and which can be used to verify their identity and contact information
  • Only votes which are submitted with a verified phone number proof are considered
  • Phone numbers, which don't belong to individuals and appear to be used for automated voting are excluded
  • IP addresses, which are associated with VPNs or datacenters and appear to be used for automated voting are excluded

Long term vision

This is an experimental project to demonstrate the feasibility of using such an approach for large scale global real time coordination. We believe effective global coordination will greatly benefit peace and increasing the chances of the long term survival of humanity.
